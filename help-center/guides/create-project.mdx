## Overview

Create and organize documentation by starting a new Brooma project.

## Steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to Dashboard">
    Login at `https://brooma.io` and open your dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Create Project">
    Click on **New Project** at the top right.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set Details">
    Fill in project name and description, then click **Create**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Documenting">
    Use the left sidebar to add pages, sections, and content to your project.
  </Step>
</Steps>
