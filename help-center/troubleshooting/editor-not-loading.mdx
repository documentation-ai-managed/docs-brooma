---
title: Editor Not Loading
description: What to do if the Brooma editor doesn't load.
---

## Symptoms

- Blank screen when opening a project
- Loading spinner persists

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Refresh the Browser">
    Press `Ctrl+R` (or `Cmd+R`) to reload the page.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear browser cache">
    Go to browser settings and clear cache/cookies. Restart browser.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Internet Connection">
    Ensure your device is online and try again.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Try Incognito Mode">
    Open Brooma in a private/incognito window.
  </Step>
</Steps>
