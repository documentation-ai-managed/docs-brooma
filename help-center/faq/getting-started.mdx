## Overview

To begin with Brooma, create a new project from your dashboard.

## Steps

1. Log into your Brooma account
2. Click the **New Project** button
3. Enter your project name and description
4. Choose a template or start blank
5. Click **Create**

Your new project will appear on your dashboard and is ready for documentation!
