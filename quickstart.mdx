## Prerequisites

<Callout kind="info" title="Before You Begin">
Ensure you have:
- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: Team member emails ready for invitations
</Callout>

## Create Your Account and Log In

Follow these steps to set up your Brooma account quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Visit the Brooma signup page at `https://app.brooma.com/signup`.

    Fill out the registration form with your email, name, and password. Brooma sends a verification email immediately.

    ```
    Example form fields:
    - Email: your.email@example.com
    - Full Name: John Doe
    - Password: (choose a strong one)
    ```
  </Step>

  <Step title="Verify and Log In" icon="check-circle">
    Check your inbox and click the verification link in the email from Brooma.

    Return to `https://app.brooma.com/login` and sign in with your credentials.

    <Callout kind="tip">
      Enable two-factor authentication during your first login for added security.
    </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Create Your First Documentation Space

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Web Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    After logging in, you'll land on the dashboard.

    Click **New Space** in the top-right corner.

    Enter details:
    - Space Name: `My Project Docs`
    - Description: `Documentation for my Brooma project`
    - Visibility: `Private` (or `Public` for sharing)

    Click **Create Space**.
  </Tab>

  <Tab title="Quick Setup" icon="zap">
    Use the one-click template:

    1. Select **From Template** during space creation.
    2. Choose `Starter Documentation` or `Team Wiki`.
    3. Customize the name and proceed.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Upload Your Initial Content

Add your first page to the space.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a New Page" icon="plus">
    In your space, click **New Page**.

    Use the editor to add content. Brooma supports Markdown, images, and embeds.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Upload Files" icon="upload">
    Drag files into the editor or use the upload button.

    ```markdown
    # Welcome to My Project

    This is your first documentation page.

    ![Project Logo](https://your-image-url.com/logo.png)
    ```
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Invite Team Members

Share your space with collaborators.

<CodeGroup tabs="Bulk Invite,Single Invite">
  ```bash
  # Copy the space invite link from the sharing menu
  https://app.brooma.com/spaces/my-project-docs/invite
  ```
  ```javascript
  // Via API (advanced)
  const response = await fetch('https://api.brooma.com/v1/spaces/invite', {
    method: 'POST',
    headers: { 'Authorization': 'Bearer YOUR_API_KEY' },
    body: JSON.stringify({
      spaceId: 'space-123',
      emails: ['team@example.com', 'colleague@example.com']
    })
  });
  ```
</CodeGroup>

Enter emails in the **Members** tab of your space settings. Set roles like `Editor` or `Viewer`.
